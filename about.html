Introduction

Leveraging Big Data Analytics to combat Illegal Mining

Surveillance Statistics Policies Impact Solutions

Illegal mining, also known as Galamsey, poses significant threats to urban sustainability, impacting environmental quality, public health, economic stability, and social structures in nearby urban areas. This act affects the physical, economic, and social well-being of numerous individuals. This project proposes the role of big data in optimizing urban sustainability by tracking the environmental degradation associated with illegal mining, such as air and water pollution, deforestation, and land misuse.

Urban sustainability is challenged by illegal mining, which leads to environmental damage, economic instability, social issues, and health risks for nearby cities.
